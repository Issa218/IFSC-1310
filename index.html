Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is how well a site works on different browsers and on different devices but what makes it different from graceful degradation is the effect it has on workflow. Progressive enhancement centers around the content of a web page since content is the main reason websites are created. In progressive enhancement the structure of a page is content out meaning content is the center of structure of what we do. The example used in the article is a peanut M&M so in this context the peanut is the content and the chocolate part is the presentation or CSS and the outer shell is the javascript.
